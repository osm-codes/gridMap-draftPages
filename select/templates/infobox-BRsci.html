Geocódigos científicos

 BR+0;  BR+1;  BR+2;  BR+3;  BR+4;  BR+5;  BR+6;  BR+7;
 BR+8;  BR+9;  BR+a;  BR+b;  BR+cm;  BR+d;  BR+eq;
 BR+fn;  BR+fp;  BR+ft.
